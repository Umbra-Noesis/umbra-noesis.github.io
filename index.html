The System

One framework. Every layer owned.

Most AI deployments are a patchwork — cloud model here, wrapper app there, third-party storage somewhere else. Umbra-Noesis owns every layer from the OS up: how it boots, how it thinks, how it talks to you, and how it connects to other nodes. Nothing is borrowed. Nothing routes through someone else's infrastructure without explicit permission.

The framework is private by design. Individual components are licensed to builders and operators who need a specific layer without adopting the full system.
